Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

FormGuard helps you check completed USCIS immigration forms for common errors before you file. This policy explains what information we collect from you, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect only what we need to deliver the service, and we do not sell your data. FormGuard is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice.

1. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to uscisformcheck.com and the FormGuard service ("we," "us," "FormGuard"). It describes how we handle information from visitors and customers ("you").

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Uploaded USCIS form images. When you submit a form for review, we receive the image or PDF you upload. These uploads frequently contain personal information including your name, date of birth, address, alien registration number (A-number), and in some forms portions of a Social Security Number. We process this information solely to generate your review.
  • Payment information. Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see, receive, or store your full credit card number. We receive only a customer ID, the amount paid, your email, and the plan you bought.
  • Communications. If you email us for support, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic request data. Our servers receive your IP address, browser user-agent, and the pages you visit. This is used for security and to fix bugs.
  • Cookies. We use a small number of strictly necessary cookies for the payment processor. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

3. How we use your information

  • To analyze your uploaded USCIS form and return an error report.
  • To process payments and send receipts.
  • To prevent abuse and protect the service (such as rate limits and fraud detection).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Who we share information with

We share data only with service providers we use to run FormGuard. We do not sell your information.

  • Anthropic — operator of the AI model that analyzes your uploaded form. The form image is sent to Anthropic's API to generate the review. Anthropic does not use your data to train its models when accessed through the API.
  • Stripe — our payment processor.
  • Netlify — our hosting provider.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information if required by law, valid court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Form images and review results are not stored on our servers after your session ends. The image is processed in memory and the report is returned to your browser.
  • Payment records are retained as required by tax and accounting law.
  • If you contact support, we retain support correspondence for up to 2 years to handle follow-ups.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices: HTTPS for all traffic, role-restricted access to service providers, and encrypted storage at our payment processor. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information safe. Because your USCIS form contains sensitive personal information, we recommend you do not upload from a public or shared network.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Access and deletion. Email support@uscisformcheck.com to request information about your data or to request deletion. Because we do not retain form images or reports, deletion of those is automatic.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA), EU/UK (GDPR), and similar laws. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or limit processing of your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights. We do not sell personal information.

8. Not legal advice

FormGuard is an AI-powered document review tool. It is not a law firm, attorney, or accredited representative. The reports we generate are informational only and not legal advice. For complex immigration matters, consult a licensed immigration attorney or a Department of Justice accredited representative.

9. Children

FormGuard is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you are submitting a form on behalf of a minor, you represent that you have legal authority to do so.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@uscisformcheck.com.
